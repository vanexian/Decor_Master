Facebook User Data Deletion Request

Welcome

This page provides information and resources for requesting the deletion of your personal data associated with our services that interact with Facebook.

Please read the information below carefully before proceeding with your data deletion request.

Important Information

  • Scope of Deletion: We will delete personal data that we have collected through your use of our Facebook-integrated application/service. This may include your profile information, activity data, and other associated content. Please note that this request does not delete your Facebook account itself; for that, you must go through Facebook's own account deletion process.
  • Processing Time: Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. We will notify you once the process is complete.
  • Irreversibility: Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please be certain before submitting a request.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Ensure you are logged into your Facebook account.
  2. Navigate to our application's settings page or use the direct link/form provided below.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to submit your data deletion request. You may be asked to verify your identity.

Alternatively, you can initiate a data deletion request by sending an email to help@matrify.ai with the subject "Facebook Data Deletion Request" and including your Facebook User ID and any other relevant information to help us identify your data.

Submit Your Request

To proceed with your data deletion request, please contact us via the email address provided above, or if applicable, use the data management tools within our application.
